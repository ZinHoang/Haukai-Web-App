Our Story

Haukai Restaurant specialises in Māori kai and is based in the Bay of Islands. It was started by a couple who are keen on creating a business that would appeal to locals and tourists visiting the area. The dinner menu features Māori and Pākeha kai options and drinks. We strive to offer unique Te Ao Māoriexperience to our customers with the use of karakia for blessing food and other Māori culture elements.
